Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is a national poet of bangladesh who is especially recogonized as Bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philoshopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazril's writtings deal with the revolution of that period of British goverment. His revolutionary attept helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and opperrission and got popularity around the Indian subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all kinds of bigotry and fundamentalism including religious, cast-based and gender-based. Nazrul wrote short stories, novels and essays but is best known for songs and poems.He pioneered new music forms such as bengali ghazas.Nazrul wrote and composed nearly 4000 songs (many recorded on HMV and gramaphone records), Collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
